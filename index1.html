Profile

Delaware Area Telangana Association (DATA) is a non-profit group that was formed by Delaware Area Telanganites to support and promote Telangana culture and identity. With headquarters in Delaware, DATA was established in 2015.

We are focused on integrating and assimilating the Telangana heritage and culture, and to address any cultural needs for telugu people in Delaware area. The group is also focused on cultural & family events, lectures, seminars, meetings, community projects and charity works etc., to strengthen the bonds among Telangana people in Delaware.

DATA is more open, transparent in structure and function. We pledge to have no religion, caste or creed and invite all the telugu people to join the group voluntarily to serve telugu people.




Delaware area Telangana Association is an association providing necessary support to the Telugu people in Delaware.
